üî• HIIT: Jump Squats & Mountain Climbers

Suggested Exercise: Jump Squats + Mountain Climbers (30 sec ON / 15 sec REST)

HIIT is a high-intensity interval training method. It quickly raises your heart rate, boosts fat burning, improves endurance, and keeps your metabolism elevated post-workout.

Jump Squats: Lower into a squat, then explode upwards. Land gently and repeat. Strengthens legs, glutes, and improves cardio.

Mountain Climbers: From a plank position, drive your knees toward your chest alternately at high speed. Great for cardio and core.
